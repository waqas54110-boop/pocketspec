Why it made our list

The U39 sits in the crowded sub-Rs. 1,500 earbuds category on Daraz, and what pushed it onto our list is the combination of Bluetooth 5.3 and ENC (Environmental Noise Cancellation) at this price point — a spec pairing that's usually reserved for earbuds twice the cost.

Buyer feedback consistently calls out call clarity as the standout feature, which lines up with the ENC hardware. Pairing speed also comes up often in reviews, with most buyers reporting a near-instant connection to their phone after the first setup.

The trade-off is battery life: expect 4–6 hours per charge on the earbuds themselves, topped up by the case. For music sessions or gaming marathons, that means a case top-up once or twice a day. Controls are touch-only, which some buyers find fiddly for volume adjustments compared to physical buttons.
