Why it made our list

At under Rs. 500, the Silm Pocket 4000mAh is aimed squarely at buyers who want a backup charge without carrying a brick in their bag. The 4000mAh cell is enough for roughly one full phone charge, which is the honest ceiling for something this size and price.

It's a newer listing on Daraz, so the review count is still building — but early feedback highlights the compact, pocket-friendly form factor as the main selling point over bulkier 10,000mAh+ options that cost 3-4x more.

If you need a power bank for a full day out or multiple device charges, this isn't it — pair it with a higher-capacity bank instead. But as an always-in-your-bag emergency top-up, the price-to-size ratio is hard to beat in this category.
